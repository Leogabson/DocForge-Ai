# Project Brief: DocForge AI

## 1. Vision & Strategy
DocForge AI is a premium, production-ready SaaS platform designed to bridge the gap between raw text and professional-grade document formatting. By leveraging AI-driven layout engines, it allows users to transform Markdown, unstyled text, or AI-generated drafts into pixel-perfect, brand-aligned documents (PDF, DOCX, HTML, etc.) in seconds.

**Market Positioning:** Competes with Notion AI, Canva Docs, and Adobe Acrobat by offering a "developer-friendly" yet "designer-quality" output.

---

## 2. Target Audience
- **Content Creators & Writers:** For rapid e-book and newsletter formatting.
- **Product Teams:** For generating professional reports, research papers, and technical documentation.
- **Business Professionals:** For creating high-stakes proposals, invoices, and contracts.
- **Developers:** For integrating formatting APIs into existing workflows.

---

## 3. Core Features & Functionality

### A. Professional Document Editor
- **Hybrid Input:** Support for Markdown, Rich Text, and AI-assisted drafting.
- **Dual-Pane Interface:** Real-time synchronized preview of the final formatted output.
- **Smart Toolbar:** Formatting tools for semantic structure (Headers, Code Blocks, Quotes).

### B. AI Formatting Assistant
- **Context-Aware Styling:** One-click transformations (e.g., "Convert this to a Research Paper style").
- **Grammar & Tone:** Professional rewriting and precision grammar checking.
- **Summarization:** Intelligent bullet-pointing and executive summary generation.

### C. Workspace & Management
- **Centralized Dashboard:** Overview of document status, storage usage, and team activity.
- **Template Library:** Industry-standard blueprints for resumes, proposals, and manuals.
- **Integrations:** Seamless syncing with Google Drive, GitHub, Notion, and Slack.

### D. Export Engine
- **Multi-Format Support:** PDF, DOCX, HTML, EPUB, RTF, and TXT.
- **Granular Customization:** Control over margins, typography, headers/footers, and watermarking.

---

## 4. Design Principles
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, elegant, and spacious. Inspired by Linear and Vercel.
- **Visual Identity:** 
  - **Primary Color:** `#4F46E5` (Indigo)
  - **Typography:** Manrope (Headings) and Inter (Body).
  - **Shape Language:** Soft corners (12-16px) and subtle glassmorphism.
- **UX Pillars:** High information density without clutter, keyboard-first navigation (Command Palette), and instant feedback loops.

---

## 5. Technical Constraints
- **Frameworks:** React/Next.js with Tailwind CSS for frontend.
- **Performance:** Sub-100ms editor latency; rapid server-side document rendering.
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance.
